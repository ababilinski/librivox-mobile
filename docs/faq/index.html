FAQ

Short answers about the app, LibriVox, privacy, and support.

Is LibriVox Mobile free?

Yes. It is completely free. There are no ads, subscriptions, paid upgrades, or paywalls.

Is this the official LibriVox website?

No. LibriVox Mobile is an independent Android app. The official volunteer project is LibriVox.org.

What can I listen to?

The app focuses on public-domain audiobooks recorded by LibriVox volunteers. You can search by title, author, chapter, or reader.

Can I listen offline?

Yes. Download books when you have a connection, then listen later without streaming.

Can I cast audiobooks?

Yes. You can cast audio to supported speakers, TVs, and Cast devices when they are available on your local network.

Do I need an account?

No. Your library, progress, bookmarks, likes, settings, cache, and downloads are stored on your device.

How do I support LibriVox?

Use the source and donation links in the app, or visit LibriVox.org to learn more about the volunteer project.

Where do I get help?

Email adrian@babilinapps.com or visit the support page.
