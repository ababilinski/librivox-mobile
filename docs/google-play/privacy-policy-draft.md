# Privacy Policy Draft

LibriVox Mobile is an unofficial app for browsing and listening to public-domain LibriVox audiobooks.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- No account is required.
- This build does not use ads or developer-run analytics.
- This build does not sell personal data.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores audiobook library items, playback progress, bookmarks, likes, app settings, search cache, cover art, and downloaded audio on your device. Android backup or device transfer may include progress and settings when enabled by your device.

## Network Use

The app contacts LibriVox and linked public audiobook hosting endpoints to search, stream, download, and load cover art. Cast playback may use your local network to play audio on a Cast receiver. Source and donation links may open in your browser.

## Permissions

- Internet and network state are used for search, streaming, downloads, cover art, and connectivity behavior.
- Foreground media playback is used for background listening.
- Notifications are used for media playback controls.
- Local network access is used for Cast playback.
