Terms of Service

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Overview

These terms apply when you use LibriVox Mobile, an unofficial Android app for finding and listening to public-domain audiobooks recorded by LibriVox volunteers.

What The App Provides

The app helps you search, stream, cast, download, and organize public-domain audiobooks. It also stores local library data such as progress, bookmarks, likes, settings, cache, cover art, and downloads.

Cost

LibriVox Mobile is completely free. The app does not sell subscriptions, in-app purchases, paid upgrades, or paid access to audiobook content.

No Account

The app does not require an account. You are responsible for your device, your local downloads, and your use of the app.

Audiobook Sources

Recordings and catalog information come from LibriVox and linked public audiobook hosting sources. LibriVox Mobile does not own those recordings and does not control their availability, metadata, or hosting.

Public-domain status can vary by country. You are responsible for following the laws that apply where you live.

Unofficial App

LibriVox Mobile is independent. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by LibriVox. The official volunteer project is at LibriVox.org.

Acceptable Use

Use the app in a reasonable way. Do not use it to interfere with LibriVox, audiobook hosts, Google Cast devices, or other services.

Third-Party Links

The app and website may link to LibriVox, audiobook hosts, donation pages, or other websites. Those sites have their own terms and privacy policies.

No Warranty

The app is provided as is. We try to keep it useful and reliable, but we do not promise that every catalog result, stream, download, Cast session, website, or source link will always work.

Changes

We may update these terms when the app changes. The updated date at the top of this page will change when that happens.
