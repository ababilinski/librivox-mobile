LibriVox Mobile helps you find and listen to public-domain audiobooks recorded by LibriVox volunteers.

Search by title, author, chapter, or reader. Stream a book, cast it, or download it for later. Your library keeps your place, bookmarks, liked books, and recent listens.

Completely free. No ads, subscriptions, or paywalls. Open source.

LibriVox Mobile is unofficial and is not affiliated with or endorsed by LibriVox.
